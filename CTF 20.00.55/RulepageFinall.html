Rules for 6174


  • The event will consist of 7 levels, each with increasing complexity.
  • Each level has a time restriction and difficulty level.
  • Level 1: 2 minutes (easy)
  • Level 2: 2 minutes (easy to moderate)
  • Level 3: 4 minutes (moderate).
  • Level 4: 5 minutes (moderate).
  • Level 5: 5 minutes (moderate to challenging).
  • Level 6: 6 minutes (difficult).
  • Level 7: 10 minutes (difficult).
  • At each stage, one GU volunteer will guide participants.
  • Participants are not allowed to view the page source at any cost.However in such cases they will be dissqualified
  • Participants must complete each level's CTF goals and submit flags to the scoring server within time limits.
  • Participants cannot cheat, hack, or sabotage the CTF infrastructure.
  • Participants must act ethically and professionally towards other teams and guides.
  • At each stage, participants will be randomly assigned one of three Stories.
  • To win the game, one person must solve all 7 levels.
  • If a player fails to complete a level, they can restart from the first level up to three times however, new participants will be favored.
  • Participants will not be permitted to use phones in the lab.
