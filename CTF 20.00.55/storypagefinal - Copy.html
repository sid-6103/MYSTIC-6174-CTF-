Rachel a teenage freelance hacker, works for companies to do thier shady, not so ethical, work for them in exchange of whooping amount of money transfered into her account. She's an adrenaline junkie, getting high off doing not so ethical things without getting caught by the "ethical hackers" of the government. She recently was landed with an offer of a lifetime, with the money being offered to her would be enough for her to live her rest of the life batheing in luxury, without needing to work for even a day later. While waiting for the company, Larsen security, to send her the information required for her to sign the contract for the offer she stumbles upon a game on the internet, darkweb. There is a game uploaded on the darkweb by the ruling family of the web, The Volkovs, along with rumours saying whoever can solve this game can secure a permanent seat in the core team working for the family. It is basically the only dream she's dreamt of ever since she got to learn about the family, to come in the eyes of the family. Although there is no particular official annoucement from the Volkovs on the site, but they are known for their notorious tectics to hire people.
