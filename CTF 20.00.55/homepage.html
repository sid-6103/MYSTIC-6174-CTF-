Welcome to MYSTIC 6174


This game aims to create a competition focused on challenging puzzles related to data security, cryptography, steganography, and internet anonymity which are secretly hidden in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites. They can be used for both competitive or educational purposes.
The Mystic 6174 will feature a series of puzzles designed to test participants' knowledge of various cybersecurity domains, including data security, cryptography, steganography, and internet anonymity. These puzzles will be deliberately cryptic, requiring critical thinking and problem-solving skills to crack. .
